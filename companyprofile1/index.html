About Us.

FIRETECH THERMAL EQUIPMENT INDIA Pvt. Ltd. formally known as SURYA FURNACES & REFRACTORIES Pvt. Ltd. , A name in the Furnace and boiler industry known since 70's for its custom designed and built furnaces and boilers for various industries. The company has served various metallurgical industries over the years with its quality products and prompt services. Customer needs are carefully studied,engineered and perfect product is offered to meet 100% results and satisfaction of its wide clientele

The company is headed and managed by qualified and experienced technocrats and professionals gained expertise through global associations and experience has come a long way in serving industry with flaw less technological solutions of Furnaces, Boilers,Flakers,Reaction vessels...etc

An integrated combination of those advantage, right from concept to commissioning is at your service... Totally committed to reliability and results.
